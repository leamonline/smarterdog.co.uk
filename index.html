Common Questions

❓ How often should my dog be groomed?

Most dogs benefit from grooming every 6-8 weeks. Long-haired breeds may need more frequent visits, while short-haired breeds can go longer between grooms.

❓ Is nail clipping included in grooming?

Yes! Nail trimming is included in our Full Groom and Maintenance Groom packages. It's also available as a standalone service for £10.

❓ Do I need an appointment?

Yes, appointments are required for all grooming services. Nail clipping is available as a drop-in service during opening hours. Book via WhatsApp for fastest response!

❓ What breeds do you groom?

We cater for all breeds! From tiny Chihuahuas to large Golden Retrievers, our 40+ years of experience means we can handle any pup with care and expertise.
