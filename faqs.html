Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our dog grooming services.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. To cancel, simply text or WhatsApp us on 07507 731 487.

  • 24+ hours notice: No charge
  • Less than 24 hours: 50% cancellation fee may apply
  • No-show: Full service charge applies

We understand that emergencies happen. If you have exceptional circumstances, please contact us and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Do you require a deposit?

For first-time customers, we may request a deposit to secure your appointment. This will be discussed when you book and is deducted from the final grooming cost.

Regular customers don't typically need to pay a deposit, as we've built a trusted relationship with them.

How long does a grooming session take?

Grooming duration varies depending on the service, breed, size, and coat condition of your dog:

  • Quick Services (Nail Trim, Ear Cleaning): 15-30 minutes
  • Bath & Brush: 1-2 hours
  • Maintenance Groom: 1.5-2.5 hours
  • Full Groom: 2-4 hours
  • De-Shedding Package: 2-3 hours

We'll give you an estimate time upon drop off, and we'll message around 15 mins before your dog will be ready for collection.

What if my dog's coat is matted or in poor condition?

If your dog's coat is severely matted, we may need to perform additional de-matting work or recommend a shorter clip for your dog's comfort and safety.

Heavy matting can cause:

  • Skin irritation and discomfort
  • Restricted movement
  • Extended grooming time

We'll always discuss options with you before proceeding, and additional charges may apply for extensive de-matting work. Regular grooming (every 6-8 weeks) helps prevent matting.

When can I bring my puppy for their first groom?

We welcome puppies from 12 weeks old (after they've had their vaccinations). The first groom is an important introduction to grooming and we keep it gentle and positive.

First puppy groom includes:

  • Getting comfortable with the grooming environment
  • Introduction to grooming tools and equipment
  • Light trim and tidy-up
  • Nail trimming and paw pad care
  • Lots of praise and treats!

Starting grooming early helps your puppy become comfortable with the process and makes future grooming sessions easier.

How do I book an appointment?

Booking is easy! You can request availability through:

  • WhatsApp: 07507 731 487
  • SMS: 07507 731 487
  • Facebook Messenger
  • Instagram Direct Message
  • Our online contact form
  • Visit us in person at 183 Kings Road, Ashton-under-Lyne, OL6 8HD

We'll confirm your appointment once we've agreed on a date and time that works for you.

What should I bring to the appointment?

Just bring your dog! We provide all grooming supplies and equipment. However, if your dog has special requirements:

  • Let us know about any medical conditions or sensitivities
  • Inform us if your dog is anxious or has behavioral concerns
  • Mention any specific grooming preferences or styles

Do you groom aggressive or anxious dogs?

We have over 40 years of experience handling dogs of all temperaments. Safety for your dog, our team, and other clients is our priority.

For anxious dogs, we:

  • Take extra time to make them comfortable
  • Use calming techniques and positive reinforcement
  • Schedule appointments at quieter times if needed

If your dog shows aggressive behavior that could pose a safety risk, we may need to stop the grooming session. We'll always discuss the situation with you and explore alternative solutions.
