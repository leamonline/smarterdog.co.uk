5. Your Responsibilities

5.1 Accurate Information

You must provide accurate and complete information about your dog, including:

  • Name, breed, age, and weight
  • Medical conditions or allergies
  • Behavioral issues (aggression, anxiety, nervousness)
  • Previous grooming experiences
  • Vaccination status

5.2 Health & Vaccinations

For the safety of all pets and staff:

  • Your dog should be in generally good health
  • We recommend up-to-date vaccinations (especially for contagious conditions)
  • Inform us of any recent illnesses, injuries, or veterinary treatments

5.3 Parasite Treatment

Your dog should be free from fleas and ticks. If we discover live parasites during grooming:

  • We will inform you immediately
  • We may need to use flea treatment products (at additional cost)
  • Additional cleaning charges may apply

5.4 Matted Coats

Severely matted coats require extra time and care:

  • We may need to clip the coat shorter than requested
  • De-matting can cause discomfort to your pet (we prioritize their welfare)
  • Additional time and charges may apply for matted coats

⚠️ Important: Your dog's welfare is our top priority. We will not proceed with any grooming that causes unnecessary distress or harm to your pet.
