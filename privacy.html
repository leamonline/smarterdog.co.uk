What Information We Collect

Contact Form

When you use our contact form to request availability or book an appointment, we collect the following personal information:

  • Your Name: First name and surname
  • Contact Details: Email address and telephone number
  • Dog Information: Your dog's name(s) and breed
  • Message: Any additional information you provide

Newsletter Subscription

When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect:

  • Email Address: To send you grooming tips, special offers, and updates
  • Consent Record: Timestamp and IP address for audit purposes
  • Subscription Status: Whether you are actively subscribed or unsubscribed

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Appointment Management: To respond to your availability requests and schedule grooming appointments
  • Service Delivery: To provide dog grooming services tailored to your pet's needs
  • Communication: To contact you about your appointments, service updates, or respond to your enquiries
  • Record Keeping: To maintain accurate records of our customers and their pets for service continuity
  • Marketing Communications (Newsletter): To send you grooming tips, special offers, seasonal promotions, and business updates (only with your explicit consent)

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate Interests: Processing is necessary for our legitimate business interests in providing grooming services and managing customer relationships
  • Consent: You provide consent when you voluntarily submit the contact form or subscribe to our newsletter
  • Contract Performance: Processing is necessary to fulfil our service agreement with you
  • Explicit Consent (Newsletter): We process your email address for marketing purposes only when you actively tick the consent checkbox and submit the newsletter form (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR)

How We Store Your Information

Contact Form Data

Your information is collected via our contact form and processed through Formspree, a third-party form handling service. Once received, your information is:

  • Stored securely in our business records (digital and/or paper-based)
  • Accessible only to authorized staff members who need it to provide our services
  • Protected by appropriate technical and organizational security measures

Newsletter Data

Newsletter subscription data is stored in Google Sheets (provided by Google Ireland Limited), which acts as our data processor:

  • Data is transmitted securely via HTTPS encryption
  • Access is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Google has Standard Contractual Clauses in place for EU data protection
  • Data is stored within the European Economic Area (EEA) where possible
  • We maintain consent records including timestamp and IP address for audit compliance

How Long We Keep Your Information

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services and maintain our customer relationship. Specifically:

  • Active Customers: We keep your information for the duration of our business relationship
  • Former Customers: We retain records for up to 3 years after your last appointment for business continuity purposes
  • Newsletter Subscribers: We keep your email address until you unsubscribe, plus 30 days for record-keeping purposes
  • Consent Records: Proof of consent (timestamp, IP address) is retained for 3 years after unsubscription for compliance purposes
  • Legal Requirements: Some records may be kept longer if required by law (e.g., accounting records)

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your personal data:

  • Formspree: Handles our contact form submissions. View their privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy
  • Google Workspace (Sheets): Stores newsletter subscription data. Google Ireland Limited acts as our data processor with Standard Contractual Clauses in place. View Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Maps: Displays our business location (no personal data collected by us through this service)
  • Google Fonts: Provides website typography (may set cookies for font delivery)

Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time (where consent is the legal basis)
